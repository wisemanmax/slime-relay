# Turn on Debrid (best quality — no server needed)

**Debrid is the easy path.** With a debrid account, SlimeWatch plays real
high-quality files straight to your device — no buffering, no ads, and **you
don't have to run a server**. It costs about **$3/month** and takes 5 minutes to
set up once.

You still get everything else (the app falls back to the shared servers
automatically for anything debrid doesn't have), so this only *adds* quality.

## Step 1 — Get a debrid account

Pick one (any works — [Real-Debrid](https://real-debrid.com/) is the usual choice
for price/value):

- **Real-Debrid** — ~$3/mo on the 6-month plan. *Note: one household at a time —
  don't share a single Real-Debrid account across different homes, or it can get
  banned. Everyone gets their own.*
- **TorBox** or **Premiumize** — a bit more, but **no one-home limit**, so a group
  can share one account.

Create the account and pay for the plan.

## Step 2 — Copy your API key

- **Real-Debrid:** sign in, then open **[real-debrid.com/apitoken](https://real-debrid.com/apitoken)**
  and copy the long code shown there.
- **TorBox:** Settings → **API** → copy your API key.
- **Premiumize:** [premiumize.me/account](https://www.premiumize.me/account) → **API Key**.
- **AllDebrid:** [alldebrid.com/apikeys](https://alldebrid.com/apikeys/) → create one → copy it.

Keep this code handy for the next step. Treat it like a password — it's tied to
your account.

## Step 3 — Enter it in SlimeWatch

**On iPhone / iPad:**
1. Open **Settings** (gear icon).
2. Under **Debrid (best quality)**, turn on **Use debrid**.
3. Pick your **Service** (Real-Debrid, TorBox, etc.).
4. Paste your **API key**.

**On Apple TV:**
1. Go to the **Settings** tab.
2. Under **Debrid (best quality)**, switch on **Use debrid**.
3. Choose your **Service** and enter the **API key** (use the on-screen keyboard,
   or type on your paired iPhone).

That's it. Play a movie or a popular show — it should start fast and look great.

## Good to know

- **It's automatic.** Debrid is tried first; if a title isn't available on it, the
  app quietly falls back to the shared servers. You can't end up worse off.
- **Movies & TV** run through debrid today. **Anime** keeps its own Sub/Dub
  sources, so your dub/sub choice always wins there.
- **One title won't play from debrid?** A few releases use a format Apple TVs and
  iPhones can't decode yet (`.mkv`) — those fall back to the servers automatically.
- **Your key stays on your device.** It isn't uploaded anywhere.
